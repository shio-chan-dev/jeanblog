# Thoughts Evaluation Article Template

Use this as the default skeleton for publishable `thoughts` posts that record a
tool, workflow, or system evaluation.

```md
---
title: "<conclusion-led title>"
subtitle: "<fit/not-fit summary>"
date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
summary: "<1-2 sentence summary>"
tags: ["..."]
categories: ["thoughts"]
keywords: ["..."]
readingTime: "about <N> min"
draft: true
---

> Core judgment: <1-2 sentence conclusion with recommendation boundary>.

## Target Readers

## Background and Motivation

## Core Concepts

## What I Expected Before Trying It

## What I Found in Actual Use

## What It Is Good For

## What It Is Not Good For

## Boundary With My Current Workflow

## A Minimal Evaluation Method for Readers

## Explanation and Tradeoffs

## Common Questions and Cautions

## Best Practices and Recommendations

## Conclusion

## References and Further Reading

## Metadata

## CTA
```

Keep the structure tight. If a section would be empty, compress it into a
nearby section rather than padding with fluff.
